Pawpalüêæ

PawPal is a full-stack web application that streamlines animal adoption and volunteer management for shelters. Users can filter and browse adoptable pets, while volunteers schedule viewings and shifts. Staff gain a comprehensive dashboard for managing animals, users, and bookings, plus real-time adoption reports. Built collaboratively with React.js, Tailwind.css, Spring Boot Java, MySQL, and AWS services (Amplify, S3, EC2), PawPal creates an efficient animal care ecosystem. I played a major role in designing the application's user-friendly frontend.
